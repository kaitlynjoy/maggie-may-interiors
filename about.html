About Maggie May

From as far back as I remember I realized that I could improve my life and those I love by making our surroundings beautiful. After some years of consulting for friends I received my degree in interior design at Dakota Technical College. For the past 14 years I have owned my own business Maggie May Interiors and worked as a interior designer in homes, retail and offices.

My experiences include designing window treatments, color consulting and full design portfolios. As a professional I understand that good design is more than how something's look and feels, it is how we function in our surroundings and the impact they have on our lives. I look forward to the opportunity to show you how powerful a fresh design can be on every aspect of your life.


By the time I leave we'll feel like old friends.
