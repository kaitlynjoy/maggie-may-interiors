Paint Colors (Interior & Exterior)

Usually the 1st step in redecorating is choosing paint colors. A beautiful background paint color in a room does marvels!

With my paint color consulting I provide you with large paint color swatches (8"x11" and 5"x9") from Benjamin Moore and Sherwin Williams. This tool helps my clients to actually be able to visualize different colors and how the color effects the space. I carry with me the best colors that the 2 companies have to offer.

Armed with my expertise in color and my client's input, we begin with taping up the large color swatches to the wall and work from there. It's fun and easy and we make good progress in short order in transitioning color from room to room. Having your colors done professionally makes all the difference!
